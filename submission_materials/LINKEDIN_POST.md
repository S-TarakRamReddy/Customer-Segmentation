Excited to share my latest project completed as part of the Synent Technologies Data Science Internship! 🚀

For Task 6, I tackled Customer Segmentation using unsupervised machine learning. By analyzing the purchasing behaviors and income levels within the Mall Customer dataset, I successfully divided the customer base into 5 distinct, actionable segments using the K-Means clustering algorithm.

Key highlights of the project:
✅ Performed comprehensive EDA to understand demographic distributions.
✅ Scaled features and utilized the Elbow Method & Silhouette Score to pinpoint K=5 as the optimal cluster count.
✅ Profiled segments like "Premium Customers" and "Budget Shoppers."
✅ Derived data-driven business recommendations to transition from generic marketing to highly targeted, ROI-focused campaigns.

This project was a fantastic deep-dive into how distance-based algorithms can unlock hidden patterns in raw data and directly translate into business value.
